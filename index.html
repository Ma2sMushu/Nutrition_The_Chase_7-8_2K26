Beat the Chaser by answering nutrition questions about carbohydrates, protein, fats, vitamins, minerals, water, balanced eating, and hauora.

Works as one file: index.html. Upload it to GitHub Pages to make it live.

How to Play

  1. Cash Builder: answer as many questions as you can before the timer ends. Each correct answer earns $1,000.
  2. Head-to-Head Chase: choose a low, middle, or high offer. Correct answers move you down the board. Wrong answers let the Chaser move.
  3. Final Chase: answer as many questions as possible. The Chaser then tries to catch your score.
  4. Restart: use the restart button anytime to replay with randomised questions.
